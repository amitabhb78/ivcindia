About Us

I.V. Communications Ltd. is a leading public limited company with an equity base of Rs. 20 Millions. The company has a trained & experienced team of Engineers & Management Professionals who have a successful track record of running and leading large Electronic Companies in India. I.V. Communications Ltd. was established in 1991. During the past 28 years, the Company has endeavored to provide the latest technology in CCTV Surveillance covering 2 - way communications facility, Image Motion Detection, Sound Splitting, Multiplexing and Remote Surveillance. The company successfully installed and commissioned surveillance system for Varanasi, Patna and Gaya airports. The surveillance system comprised of IP cameras along with network switches, duplex and single mode Media converters and interconnection of these systems with their Servers using both optic-fibre communications cables as well as CAT 6 cables. 

In addition, the company has been gradually adding and supplying Automatic Fire Detection, Fire Fighting & Fire Extinguishing Systems, Perimeter Protection Systems, Access Control Systems, Boom Barrier/Flap Barrier, Automation Gates for last several years and has recently added Low Power Walkie-Talkie in license free frequency zone of 865 MHz to 867 MHz. From 2014, company has also started providing Electronic Consultancy Services in the area of Electronic Surveillance and other Electronic systems and has recently completed a consultancy project for Mangalore Special Economic Zone (MSEZ) spread over in area of 1800 Acres in the state of Karnataka.
